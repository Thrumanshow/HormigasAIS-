## 1. Purpose and Nature of This Document

This document defines the governance model of the HormigasAIS ecosystem.

Its purpose is to establish clear, enforceable, and verifiable boundaries regarding authority, representation, contribution, and sovereignty across all repositories, protocols, nodes, implementations, and derivative works associated with HormigasAIS.

This document is **normative**, not descriptive.  
Its provisions override informal interpretations, community assumptions, marketing language, and third-party representations.

---

## 2. Definition of Governance Scope

Governance within HormigasAIS applies to:

- Source code repositories
- Protocol specifications (including LBH)
- Documentation and architectural designs
- Nodes, adapters, and implementations
- Organizational identity and naming
- Use of the HormigasAIS name, symbols, or narrative

Governance **does not imply ownership** over external forks, experiments, or private implementations unless explicitly stated in writing by the Maintainer of Record.

### 3.2 Non-Transferability of Authority

Authority within HormigasAIS is **non-transferable by default**.

The following actions **do not grant authority**:

- Forking a repository
- Contributing code
- Maintaining a fork
- Referencing HormigasAIS
- Mirroring repositories
- Community activity or endorsement

Any delegation of authority must be **explicit, written, traceable, and intentional**.

---

## 4. Forks, Nodes, and Authority (Critical Distinction)

For the avoidance of doubt:

- A **fork is not a node**
- A **node is not authority**
- **Authority is never inherited**

### 4.1 Forks

Forks are technically permitted under the applicable license.

However, a fork:

- Is an independent derivative work
- Has no representational rights
- Cannot claim alignment, endorsement, or equivalence
- Must not present itself as an official HormigasAIS component

### 4.2 Nodes

Nodes are **conceptual and architectural entities**, not repositories.

A valid node requires all of the following:

- Explicit recognition by the Maintainer of Record
- A defined role within the ecosystem
- Governance alignment
- Validation mechanisms

Absent these conditions, an implementation is considered an **experiment**, not a node.

## 5. Contribution Model

### 5.1 Nature of Contributions

Contributions are accepted under the following assumptions:

- Contributions are technical, not political
- Contributions do not imply ownership
- Contributions do not imply governance rights
- Contributions may be modified, rejected, or deprecated
- Submission of a contribution constitutes acceptance of this governance model

### 5.2 Contribution ≠ Representation

No contributor may:

- Speak on behalf of HormigasAIS
- Issue statements as an authority
- Define roadmap, doctrine, or policy
- Bind the ecosystem through external communication

## 6. Organizational Identity and Representation

HormigasAIS operates as a **technical and conceptual organization**, not as a public authority or legal proxy unless explicitly stated elsewhere.

- The GitHub organization account is an infrastructure boundary
- Social presence does not confer governance status
- External platforms do not define internal authority

Any public or private claim of representation without authorization is considered **invalid**.

## 10. Gobernanza y Autoridad — Cláusula de Sellado (ES)

HormigasAIS se rige por un modelo de gobernanza explícito, documentado, verificable y vinculante, diseñado para preservar la soberanía técnica, conceptual y operativa del ecosistema.

Este repositorio permite la bifurcación (fork), el uso y la creación de trabajos derivados exclusivamente conforme a los términos de su licencia aplicable.

Ninguna acción técnica —incluida, sin carácter limitativo, la visibilidad, participación, contribución, colaboración o bifurcación— confiere, implica ni presume delegación alguna de autoridad, representación, legitimidad institucional o reconocimiento como nodo oficial del ecosistema HormigasAIS.

La creación de forks, adaptaciones o trabajos derivados no constituye, por sí misma ni de forma implícita, reconocimiento como nodo oficial, entidad gobernante, órgano decisorio ni representación legítima del ecosistema HormigasAIS.

La autoridad, los roles, los límites de actuación, los criterios de legitimidad y los procesos de validación se encuentran definidos de manera exclusiva, expresa y vinculante en este documento, que constituye la única fuente normativa en materia de gobernanza del ecosistema.

## 11. Governance and Authority — Mirror Seal Clause (EN)

HormigasAIS is governed by an explicit, documented, verifiable, and binding governance model, designed to preserve the technical, conceptual, and operational sovereignty of the ecosystem.

This repository permits forking, use, and the creation of derivative works strictly in accordance with its applicable license.

No technical action —including, without limitation, visibility, participation, contribution, collaboration, or forking— grants, implies, or presumes any delegation of authority, representation, institutional legitimacy, or recognition as an official node of the HormigasAIS ecosystem.

The creation of forks, adaptations, or derivative works does not, by itself or implicitly, constitute recognition as an official node, governing entity, decision-making body, or legitimate representative of the HormigasAIS ecosystem.

All use, contribution, forking, or derivation of this repository is subject to and interpreted in accordance with this governing framework, which prevails over any external, implied, or unauthorized interpretation.
